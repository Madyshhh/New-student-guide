Everything you need to know about interest groups!

No matter what you’re into, our 120+ clubs, societies and sports clubs are bound to offer something that piques your interest – from athletics to anime, volleyball to videogames. And don't worry, if you really can’t see a club that takes your fancy, we're happy to support you in starting your own.

Where to find interest groups:

You can find more than 120 clubs if you click here.

How to join:

When you find the club you are interested in, you will need two things: A Clubs & Socs Membership and Individual Clubs Membership.
A Clubs & Socs Membership is a membership that enables you to join any of the Clubs and Societies in Your Union.

The Clubs & Socs Membership costs £5 and runs from June 1st to May 31st.
Individual club membership price varies for each club so please have a look on the current clubs list to find a club you want to join, prices and joining instructions.

When and where:

Each club has a time and place they meet up, you should be available to find this in the club description, or by speaking with a member of the club.
