Apps you will need throughout university!

Supporting your studies with increased teaching on campus and providing the right teaching tools to support your learning experience is our priority.

Here is an explanation of the apps you will need to use througout your time in Stirling.

Canvas:

In addition to on campus teaching, you will learn through our virtual learning environment, Canvas. This is a supportive, inclusive and active space for learning. Canvas will lead you through your module material and activities, to help keep you on track. Most live teaching sessions (online or on campus) will be recorded using ‘Listen Again’, so you can use these recordings to catch up and revise.

Canvas can be accessed through browser, and it can be dowloaded from the app store!

It is essential for you, as lecturers will be providing materials and will keep you updated about modules, on Canvas.

University app:

Get all your university services at your fingertips with our new University of Stirling app. The University of Stirling app provides real-time, on-the-go, anytime information to support you during your time at Stirling. It is available to all students and staff for free.

App features include:
Digital student or staff card
Book gym and exercise classes
Order and pay for food on campus
Book library study space
Log into Canvas and University email easily
Plus much more!

We know everyone’s experience at University is different, which is why we’ve given you the option to tailor the app to your own preferences. Simply tap the menu then drag the tiles around the screen to prioritise your favourite features. You can also switch off tiles you’re not interested in.
